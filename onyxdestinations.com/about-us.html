About Us

Discover. Book. Experience.
At Onyx Destinations, we’re redefining the way India travels. Based in Odisha, we connect travelers with handpicked hotels and unforgettable experiences across destinations. Our mission is simple — to make every journey smooth, affordable, and memorable. With trusted partners and 24×7 support, your next getaway begins with Onyx.

Our Mission

To simplify travel by providing verified, budget-friendly, quality accommodations coupled with transparent service, easy booking, and round-the-clock support.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted and preferred hotel booking platform — connecting every traveler to a comfortable, reliable stay in every corner of the country.

What We Do

Wide Network of Properties — From budget guesthouses to boutique hotels and premium resorts, we offer a carefully curated portfolio.

Easy Booking Experience — Search, compare, and confirm, all in a few simple steps.

Transparent Pricing — No hidden fees, no unpleasant surprises.

Secure Payments — Multiple safe payment options for peace of mind.

24/7 Support — Our team is available to assist with everything from booking queries to check-ins and refunds.
