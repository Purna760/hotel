 Stay Line-PR007OX – Simplicity & Comfort in the Holy Land of Puri ✨

Nestled in the spiritual city of Puri, Stay Line-PR007OXoffers a homely and budget-friendly stay . With neat and cozy rooms , caring hospitality , and a peaceful environment, it is perfect for pilgrims, families, and travelers who want a simple yet comfortable experience.

Located within easy reach of the iconic Jagannath Temple and close to the serene beaches , the lodge serves as a convenient base for both devotion and leisure. After exploring the holy city — from temple rituals to vibrant markets — guests can return to a quiet, relaxing stay.
