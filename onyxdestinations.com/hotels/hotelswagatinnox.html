Stay Line-KDP001OX – Your Friendly Stay at Kendrapada 

True to its name, Stay Line-KDP001OX welcomes every guest with warmth and simplicity. Situated in Kendrapada, this hotel offers clean and cozy rooms, a homely atmosphere, and caring hospitality that ensures a comfortable stay.

Whether you’re in Kendrapada for pilgrimage, business, or leisure, the hotel serves as a convenient stopover. The famous Baladevjew Temple is within easy reach, offering a glimpse into Odisha’s deep spiritual heritage. For nature lovers, the unique ecosystem of Bhitarkanika National Park lies not too far, making it easy to explore mangroves, wildlife, and adventure.

After a day of exploration, return to Swagat Inn to relax in peace ✨, enjoy local flavors, or simply unwind in a cozy setting. Perfect for families, pilgrims, and casual travelers, this hotel makes every stay simple yet memorable.

At Stay Line-KDP001OX Ichhapur, hospitality is not just service — it’s a feeling of belonging ❤️.
